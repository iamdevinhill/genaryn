About Us

At Genaryn, we are committed to transforming the way commanders make decisions in complex, high-stakes environments. Modern conflicts evolve faster than traditional planning cycles can accommodate, and commanders are often forced to rely on incomplete information, limited staff resources, and human judgment under extreme pressure. That's where we come in.

Genaryn's AI Deputy Commander is built to act as a trusted strategic thought partner, empowering leaders with insights drawn from both the deep well of military history and the rapidly evolving dynamics of today's battlespace. Unlike generic AI tools, our platform is purpose-built for defense and national security missions, ensuring operational relevance, resilience, and ethical alignment with military standards.

We see AI not as a replacement for human leadership, but as a force multiplier for human judgment. By fusing advanced machine learning with the timeless principles of command and control, Genaryn helps leaders see farther, react faster, and decide with confidence.

Leadership

Jason Warren

Founder and Chief Executive Officer

Genaryn

Jason W. Warren, PhD. is a West Point graduate (1999), retired Army Lieutenant Colonel, and former Assistant Professor of History. Dr. Warren served for over twenty years, including two deployments and an overseas tour, and is a recipient of the Bronze Star Medal. He earned his PhD. and MA at The Ohio State University.

Dr. Warren's academic publications include Connecticut Unscathed: Victory in the Great Narragansett War (The University of Oklahoma Press, 2014), Drawdown: The American Way of Postwar (New York University Press, 2016), The Many Faces of War (Marymount Institute Press, 2018), and Landpower in the Long War: Projecting Force After 9/11 (University Press of Kentucky, 2019). He was a Senior Subject Matter Expert with Booz Allen Hamilton's Department of Defense consulting business for six years. Dr. Warren is co-founder of Genaryn, the AI products of which utilize his deep knowledge of military history and practical military experience.

As Chief Executive Officer of Genaryn, Dr. Warren leverages his leadership experience, strategic insight, and historical expertise to guide the company's mission of delivering cutting-edge AI solutions for defense, government, and industry. His career has been defined by translating complex challenges into actionable strategies, a skill he now applies to shaping Genaryn's vision, cultivating partnerships, and driving innovation at the intersection of technology and national security.
